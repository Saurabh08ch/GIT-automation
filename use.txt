Use it from Git Bash (not PowerShell):

cd /c/shell/shellbash
./git_auto_push.sh
Useful options:

./git_auto_push.sh --interval-sec 300
./git_auto_push.sh --message-prefix "auto: sync"
./git_auto_push.sh --log-file "$HOME/git-auto-push-shellbash.log"
Quick test (faster loop):

./git_auto_push.sh --interval-sec 30
